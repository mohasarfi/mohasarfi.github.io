ML-based Analog Circuit Design

AT WORK
actively building at AsterQuanta

Leading research on RL and generative models for automated analog circuit design at AsterQuanta.

from topology generation to multi-objective optimization!

The core challenge is that generating a circuit topology and optimizing its parameters cannot be treated as two separate steps; they are deeply coupled. The research tackles this as a unified problem using a bi-level framework, where a generative model proposes circuit structures and an RL agent handles device-level sizing across multiple competing objectives.

CareWatch

ACTIVE
actively building

A fall detection and automated report generation system. Computer vision detects fall events from video; an LLM-driven pipeline then generates structured incident reports, reducing the manual burden on caregivers and improving response times.

Targeting care facilities and assisted living environments where real-time monitoring and documentation are both critical and understaffed.
